      Welcome to the india's 8th largest state, Andhra Pradesh. Andhra has enormous amount of tourist places to visit, here we will mention some of important tourist places that one should visit. It has famous temples like Tirumala Venkateswara temple , Kalahasthi temple, Gnana Saraswati temple, Bradhrachalam temple, etc. It has ancient building likes Charminar, Golkonda, Chowmahanalla palace,Qutub Shahi tomb, Kondaveedu fort etc. It has Nerhu zoological park, Indria Gandhi zoological park and Salar Jahar museum, Brila Mandir ,Brila science museums, Undavalli caves and Nizam museum
